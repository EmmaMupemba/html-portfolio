Best movies in recent memory

My top 3 movies from 2024:

  • The Holdovers

    Genre: Comedy
    Duration: 133 min
    Highlights: Very nostalgic and reminiscent of movies from the 90's such as Dead Poets society and Good will hunting. Truly touching and such a demonstration that not everything needs to be a remake to be good

  • It's what's inside

    Genre: Comedy, Thriller, Mystery
    Duration: 103 min
    Highlights: I loved this movie, basically it's like mafia a game where people have an oppurtunity to walk a short short mile in the shoes of their friends. The movie is fresh and explores themes such as jeolousy and comparisons in friendships. Additionally you really cannot decisde who you are rooting for. One other thing I appreciated is that the end is not polished and presented in a neat little bow, the ending really leaves wondering where does the lives of the charecters go to from here.

  • Inside Out 2

    Genre: Animation, Adventure
    Duration: 96 min
    Highlights: I would have to think really hard to identify a francshice where the sequel is just as good as the original. Inside Out was released in 2015, 9 years later and as users we currently find ourselves deep in trenches that is Hollywoods aversion to risk resulting in hoards and hoards of sequals and remakes of past Blockblasters. I Had the oppurtunity to watch Inside Out 2 in cinemas and can clearly recall the moment when we reached the climax of the movie, practically everyone in the theatre was holding in their breath as if any sound from them would immortalise the moment. In maybe not my finest moment I could not stop laughing (Ovbiously very quietly) but the moment was so intense and so real that the tension literally brought me to a breaking point. Excellent move might even be my favourite movie of 2024
